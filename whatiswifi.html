What is Wifi?

Within today’s modern society we often take for granted the ability to use and connect to the internet wirelessly allowing us to be more mobile and productive in our workspaces and homes. Wireless Fidelity more commonly known as Wifi, is the technology which allows multiple devices such as our phones, laptops and tablets to connect to the internet without the use of a physical cable.

Wifi works similar to our phones. Wifi uses radio waves to transmit information across a wireless network. A device known as a wireless transmitter is required which receives information from the internet via your home internet connection this device is also known and referred to a your “Wireless Access Point or WAP this information is then converted into radio waves and is emitted allowing our devices to connect to the internet Wirelessly.
